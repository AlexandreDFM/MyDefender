This is the how to play menu.

This game is a reproduction of Bloons TD 5.

The objective of the game is to kill Bloons with Monkey equipped by Darts.
You can place a monkey were you want if it's logical, not on the road, or trees.
Bloons follow the road, if they arrives to the end of the road you lost a live.
If you have no more life, you have lost.
Be the best Monkey by killing all the bloons !
